Rules of GridMasters

GridMasters is an interactive simulation based on Conway's Game of Life

  • Each cell can either be alive or dead.
  • Each cell interacts with its 8 neighboring cells.
  • Any live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors survives to the next generation.
  • Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes a live cell (birth).
  • All other live cells die in the next generation (overpopulation or loneliness).
  • The rules repeat over generations, creating dynamic patterns over time.
  • Examples include still lifes, oscillators, spaceships, and glider guns.
